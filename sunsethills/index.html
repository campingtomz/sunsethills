Factorial Exercise

  • A factorial is represented by the Symbol "!". It will multiply the input number by each proceeding whole number until 1 is reached.
  • The factorial equation is:

  • n! = n(n-1)(n-2)....(2)(1)

  • The factorial of 0 is 1
  • You generally can not take the Factorial of non-whole and negative numbers
  • The factorials of negative numbers are complex Numbers more information in the links below
  • Examples:
  • 5! = 5x4x3x2x1 = 120
  • 7! = 7x6x5x4x3x2x1 = 5,040
  • 10! = 10x9x8x7x6x5x4x3x2x1 = 3,628,800
